Details in the Text

He tried to feel his way through the darkness and froze: apparently, there was no other exit from this small courtyard except the passage he had entered through. The footsteps grew louder and louder, and he saw a dark figure round the corner. Desperately, his eyes searched through the night for a way out. Was it really all over now? Were all the effort and preparation in vain? He pressed himself tightly against the wall behind him and hoped the pursuer wouldn’t see him — when suddenly a door next to him creaked softly as it swung back and forth in the night wind.
